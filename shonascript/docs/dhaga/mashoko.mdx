# Mashoko


## Musoro we nyaya
Zvinhu zvese zvinobuda zvakanyorwa pa website yako, ndizvo zvinenge zvatariswa semashoko ne `dhaga`. 
Unogona kutongoisa `mashoko` pawebsite yako uchitongonyora zvaukuda kunyora zvacho zvisiri mu`box` sezvatakaita pama flag eNigeria ne France kumashure


Kazhinji kana pachinyorwa nyaya, panowanzoiswa mashoko mahombe anenge ari emusoro we nyaya (_heading_), nyaya yacho yozonyorwa nemashoko madiki. 

Kana uchida kuisa musoro we nyaya pa website yako, unoshandisa `mn`. `Size` yemusoro wenyaya inobva pana 1 kusvika pana 6. Ma `size` aya anobva ku 1 achienda ku 6 achiita madiki, saka `mn1` muhombe kudarika `mn6`. 

<DuoImage source1="/img/mn1.jpg" source2="/img/mn1.1.jpg"/>

:::info chii chikuitika apa
- Taisa misoro yenyaya `mn` yakasiyana ma size akasiyana kubva ku 1 kusvika ku 6
    - `mn1` muhombe kupfuura `mn6` sezviri kuoneka
:::

Unogona kuchinja `color` yemashoko ari mu `mn` uchishandisa `ms-c-` zvichireva kuti `mashoko color`. 

:::success Zvekuita
- Nyora zita rako mu `mn1`
- Chinja `color` kuita chero imwe `color` kunze kwe black
- Ita kuti Zita rako riende pakati
:::

<DuoImage source1="/img/mn2.jpg" source2="/img/mn2.1.jpg"/>

:::info chii chikuitika apa
- Ndaisa `mn1` ane `color` ye `#008080` na `ms-c-#008080`, ndobva ndaronga mashoko kuti aenda pakati na `rg-c`
    - ma `color` aya ari kumashure kwekupedzisira kwe book, sarudzawo yaunofarira ikoko

- Pandaisa `mn` chete pa website, abva aenda pamusoro soro pa `screen` asi inini ndanga ndichida kuti ati dzikei zvishoma, saka ndazoisa `box` risina chinhu pamusoro kuti `mn` adzike pasi
:::

## Mashoko mu box
Takamboona kumashure kuti kana uchida kungonyora mashoko anobuda pa website, unotongonyora mu `code` yako zvakangodaro. Ukangonyora mashoko ari pachena, asiri mubox, hauzokwanise kuchinja zvinhu zvakaita se `color`, `size`, kana `ukobvu` hwe mashoko aya.
Saka kuti ugone kuzochinja mamiriro e mashoko ako, unofana kumaisa mu `box`. Kana waisa mashoko mu`box`, unokwanisa kuchinja izvi:

|Nickname |Zvazvinoreva | Muenzaniso|
|---|---|---|
|ms-c |color ye mashoko | `ms-c-red` anochinja mashoko kuita `red`|
|ms-sz| size ye mashoko | `ms-sz-2` anochinja size yemashoko kuita 2. Masize aya anobva ku 1 kusvika ku 10|
|ms -uk | ukobvu hwemashoko | `ms-uk-3` anochinja ukobvu hwemashoko kuita size 3. Masize aya anobva ku 1 kusvika ku 4|

<DuoImage source1="/img/mn3.jpg" source2="/img/mn3.1.jpg"/>

:::info chii chikuitika apa
- Taumba ma `box` 4 ane ma `color` atatora kubva kwekupedzisira kwe bhuku
- Tachinja ukobvu hwemashoko aya tichishandisa `ms-uk` 
:::

:::success Zvekuita 1
- chinja `size` yemashoko ari mu `box` uchishandisa `ms-sz-` sokuti `ms-sz-3` uone zvinoitika. 
- Chinja ma `size` aya kusvika ku 10 kana uchida kuona ese 
:::
