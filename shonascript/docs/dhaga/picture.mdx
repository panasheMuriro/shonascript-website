# Picture

Kumashure uku taona kuti `box` rinogona kupihwa `color` dzakasiyana siyana kuti rioneke. Unogonawo kurega kuripa `color`, asi woripa `picture` inozadza `box` racho.
Mu application ye ShonaScript mune ma picture 6 arimo kare aunogona kushandisa. Unogonawo kuwedzera ako wega anobva mu phone mako kuti uzoshandisa.

Pane bhatani riripazasi kuright rine ma`line`3, ndiro raunoshandisa kuenda kuma `picture` kana kudzoka kubva ikoko
<DuoImage source1="/img/pic1.jpg" source2="/img/pic1.1.jpg"/>

Unoona kuti une 6 ma `picture` ane ma number kubva ku 1 kusvika ku 6. Unogonawo kuisawo ma `picture` ako kana ukabaya bhatani rakanzi `upload picture`. Kana waisa picture rinobva m phone, uchaona kuti picture iroro richapihwa nhamba 7, ma nhamba iwayo ndiwo aunoshandisa kuisa picture pa `box`uchishandisa `pic-` seizvi:

```
box pic-1:
;
```
<DuoImage source1="/img/pic2.jpg" source2="/img/pic2.1.jpg"/>

:::info chii chikuitika apa
- Taumba `box` rine `size 5`
- Tashandisa `pic-2` tichitora kubva pane ma `picture` 6 awakapihwa kare.
    - Ukaenda kuma `pictures`, unoona kuti `picture` ine nhamba 2 ndiyo yabuda pa `website` yedu     
:::

Kana uchinge wafunga kushandisa ma picture anobva muphone yako, unokurudzirwa kuita uchibvisa mamwe ma picture aunenge usingashandise ucibaya bhatani rakanzi `bvisa`.
Izvi zvinoitirwa kuti application iyi isaremerwe nema `picture` yozotadza kunyatsoshanda mushe. Unogona kuti ukaona nhamba yema `picture` ako yapfuura kana `15` otanga kubvisa mamwe.

:::success Zvekuita
- Umba `box` rine rakareba `kr-5` uye rakafara `kf-5`
- Ita kuti `box` iri riite `circle` uchibvisa makona
- Chiisa `picture` rako iwewe pa `box` iri 
- Pazasi pacho nyora zita rako
:::

Unogona kuda kuti zita rako rawanyora iri riite rihombe, kana kuti mamwe mashoko aite madiki kana kuti ave neukobvu hwakasiyana siyana. Ipapo panoshanda mashoko `ms-`, sezvauchaona pa topic ikutevera
