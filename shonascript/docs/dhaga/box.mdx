# Box
Ma `shapes`, ma `pictures`, nezvimwe zvakawanda zvaunoona pama `website` zvinenge zviri muma `box`. 
Tichashandisa website ye **Econet** kuti tione kuti ma `box` arikutaurwa akaita sei.
Picture iri ku left iri kuratidza website ye Econet semaonero aunoita iwewe kana ukaendapo uchishandisa phone yako. 
Picture iri kuright iri kuratidza ma box akashandiswa kugadzira website iyi. 
Uchazoziva zvakawanda zvine chekuita nema `box` aya kana ukaramba uchiverenga.

## Color ye Box
Zvataona kuti website inogadzirwa nema `box`, taakuda kuumbawo website yedu ne `Dhaga`, tobva taisa `box` rinooneka pa website iyoyo.

```
box:
;
```

:::info **chii chikuitika apa?**

- Tagadzira box, tobva tavhura box racho na `:`, tobva tazorivhara na `;`. 
- `Box` iri harina chinhu, apa tikuda kungoona kuti rakaita sei.
- Ukanyora code iyi mu `application` wobva wabaya bhatani re `play`,
panobuda peji rewhite risina chinhu. 

Izvi zvinoitika nekuti `box` 
rawaisa pa peji ye website yako harina `color`. 
Tikuda kuchinja `color` ye `box` kuita `green` kuti rioneke `box` racho. 
Kuchinja color yebox, unoshandisa `c-` seizvi:

```
box c-green:
;
```
:::

<DuoImage source1="/img/box1.jpg" source2="/img/box1.1.jpg"/>

:::info **chii chikuitika apa**

- Taumba `box`, tobva taudza `Dhaga` kuti `color` ye `box` iri ngaiite `green`. 
- Ma `color` mamwe aunogona kushandisa ndeanoti:
    `red, green, blue, yellow, purple, black, white, gray`. Panozoitawo mamwe ma color anosvika **1486** 
 anoonekwa pa peji rekupedzisira anotanga na `#`
- Ukatarisa ka `box` kedu aka, unoona kuti kakareba uye kakafara zvakangofanana, nemamwe mashoko, tiri kuti ka `square`.
:::

## Size ye Box
Pese paunoumba `box` ne `Dhaga`, rinotanga rakareba `size 1` uye rakafara `size 1`. 
Ukatarisa pamusoro apa pataisa `color` ye `box`, tashandisa `c-`. 
Tikushandisa ma `abbreviation` kana kuti ma `nicknames` ezvinhu zve `box` kuti tisanetsekane pakunyora `code` yedu. 
Munhu anonzi `Takudzwa` anogona kupihwa nickname yekuti `tk`, kuti zviite nyore pakudaidza zita iri.
Tichashandisa ma `nickname` akawanda pakuumba ma `website` ne `Dhaga`.

Kana uchida kuchinja kureba kwakaita box, unoshandisa `nickname` ya kureba `kr-`. `Box` rinotanga rakareba `kr-1`, zvichireva kuti `kureba size 1` sezvambotaurwa kumashure. 
Kuchinja kufara kwakaita `box`, unoshandisa `kf-`, saka `box` rinotanga rakafara `kf-1`.
`Size` yekufara `kf-` inobva pana `0` kuzvika pana `10`.`Size` yekureba `kr-` haina magumo, asi unofana kuziva kuti haumboshandise ma nhamba akakurisa zvekusvika kuna ana `kr-100`. 
Unobvumidzwa kushandisa ma nhamba akaita se `1.5`. `1,5` haabvumidzwe.
Nemamwe mashoko, tiri kuti `one point five` anobvumidzwa, asi `one comma five` haabvumidzwe. 

:::success Zvekuita
1. chinja `kureba` kuita `4`
2. chinja `kufara` kuita `3`
3. chinja `color` kuita `purple`
:::

- Handina kunyora kureba `kr-` nekuti ndikuda kuti kureba kwacho kuite `size 1`
    - sezvambotaurwa kumashure uku, ukasaisa `kr-`, kana `kf-`, box rako rinenge riine `kr-1` kana `kf-1`

## Zvinhu mu Box

Pese patagadzira ma `box` kumashure tanga tisingaise chinhu mukati imomo. 
Taakuda kugadzira box, tobva taisa rimwe box mukati me box iroro.

:::success Zvekuita
- gadzira `box` rine zvinhi izvi:
    - color yaunoda
    - `kf-3`
    - `kr-3`
- isa `box` mukati me box rawagadzira rine zvinhu izvi:
    - color yakasiyana
    - `kf-2`
    - `kf-2`
- mu `box` remukati, isa `mashoko` ekuti `dhaga`
:::

<DuoImage source1="/img/box5.jpg" source2="/img/box5.1.jpg"/>


:::info chii chikuitika apa
- Taumba `box` hombe rine size `kf-3` ne `kr-3` ne `c-green`
- Taisa `box` diki mukati me `box` rekutanga re `c-red`
    - `Box` iri rinoenda pakati pekureba kwakaita mu`box` mariri, uye rinenge raka`rongwa` ku `left`. Uchazoziva kumberi kuti unorongasei zvakasiyana neizvi.
- Taisa `mashoko` mukati me `box` diki. 
    - ukangonyora mashoko asinei nekuumba `box`, anongoonekwawo se `mashoko` anongobudawo pa website asina zvaari kuumba.
:::


:::danger Size ye zvinhu mu`box`

- Size ya zvinhu zviri mu`box` inofana kuita diki pane `size` ye `box` racho.
- Kana uine `box` rakareba `kf-3`, obva waisa `box` mukati rakareba `kf-4`, `box` iri rinopfuurira, izvi zvinokanganisa kurongeka kwe `website` yako.
   - Tarisa `picture` iri pazasi apa uone zviri kutsanangurwa apa 
:::

### Kuronga Zviri mu Box
Unoshandisa `rg-` kuronga zvinhu zviri mu`box`. Ukasashandisa `rg-`, zvinhu zviri mu`box` zvinongrongwa zvakawandira ku `left`.

| Nickname |Zvazvinoreva| Zvinoitika|
| ---       | ---        |---       |
|rg-c       | ronga center | zvese zvinoenda pakati |
|rg-l       | ronga left | zvese zvinoenda kuleft |
|rg-r       | ronga right | zvese zvinoenda kuright |
|rg-sp       | ronga space | zvinhu zviri mubox zvinopanana space yakaenzana |

<DuoImage source1="/img/box10.jpg" source2="/img/box10.1.jpg"/>

:::info chii chikuitika apa
- Taumba ma `box` 4 e `blue` ane mamwe ma `box` 2 e `red` ne `yellow`.
- Taronga zviri mukati macho zvakasiyana siyana tichishandisa `rg-l`, `rg-c`, `rg-r`, na `rg-sp` 
:::

### Size ye zviri mu Box
`Box` rihombe rakafara `size 3`, kochii chinoitika kana size yekufara kwakaita ma `box` emukati iri hombe kudarika mu `box` maari?

<DuoImage source1="/img/box8.jpg" source2="/img/box8.1.jpg"/>

:::info chii chikuitika apa
- `box` rihombe rine `kf-3`
- ma `box` arimukati ane `kf-2` ese, zvichireva kuti ukasanganisa kufara kwaakaita ese ari two, `kf` inobva yaita 4, asi 4 muhombe pana 3.
- kana izvi zvaitika, `dhaga` inoedza kuronga zviri mukati me box, kuti chimwe chiende pa musoro, chimwe pazasi, zvingodaro
    - marongerwo arikuedza kuitwa apa ndeekuti kufara kwema `box` aya kukwane mu `box` rihombe iro
:::

Zvataona kuti mabox ari mukati merimwe `box` anorongwa sei, taakudzokera kunogadzira mamwe ma flag. Tikuda kugadzira 2: ye Nigeria, neye France

:::success Zvekuita
- Gadzira flag ye Nigeria
- Gadzira Flag ye France
:::

<DuoImage source1="/img/box9.jpg" source2="/img/box9.1.jpg"/>

:::info chii chikuitika apa
- tanyora kuti Flag ye Nigeria pa `line 1`, aya mashoko saka anongobuda akadaro pa `website`
- Taumba `box` re white rine `kf-9`, tobva taisa ma `box` 3 mukati ane `kf-3` rimwe ne rimwe. Tikasanganisa 3+3+3 tinowana 9, saka ma `box` aya ari 3, anobva angokwana achizadza `box` raari
- kureba kwema `box` emukati tangoitawo kuti kufanane ne `box` raari
:::

### Kona ye Box

Unokwanisa kubvisa makona e `box` kuti aite `round` uchishandisa `kn-`. `Size` yacho ya `kn-` inobva ku `0` kusvika ku `10`. Kana uine `box` re `square`, `kn-10` anoita kuti `box` re `square` riite `circle`

<DuoImage source1="/img/box11.jpg" source2="/img/box11.1.jpg"/>

:::info chii chikuitika apa
- Taumba ma `box` ane `size` yakafanana nema `color` akasiyana
- Tabvisa ma `kona` na `kn-` tichishandisa ma `size` akasiyana siyana
    - `box` rekupedzisira rabva ratoita `circle` nekuti tabvisa ma `kona` ese ne `size` yekupedzisira `10`
:::

**Kubvisa ma kona ndizvo zvimwe zvandofarira kuita kana ndichigadzira ma `website`.**

## Mumvuri we box
Ma `website` akawanda enguva ino anenge aine ma `box` nema `bhatani` ane `mumvuri`(shadow) anoita kunge akasimudzwa. Izvi zvinoita kuti `website` yako ibude zvakanaka ichiitawo kunge yakagadzirwa nguva ino, pane kuti iite kunge yakagadzirwa internet ichangotanga.
Unoshandisa `mv-` kuisa `mumvuri` pa`box`. `Size` yemumvuri we `box` inotanga pa `0` kusvika pa `10`.

<DuoImage source1="/img/box12.jpg" source2="/img/box12.1.jpg"/>

:::info chii chikuitika apa
- taumba `box` rine `size 4`, re `white`
- taisa mumvuri we `size 6` pa box iri
    - zvinogona kusanyatsooneka zvakanaka pa `picture` iyi, asi ekatarisisa kumiganhu ye `box` iri unoona `color` inenge `gray`, ndiwo mumvuri wacho
:::

## Muganhu we box

Unogona kuisa muganhu pa `box` wobva wachinja `color` yacho yemuganhu uchiedza kuda kuti box rako ribudewo zvakanaka. 
Kuisa muganhu unoshandisa `mg-sz-`, zvichireva kuti `muganhu size`.
`Size` yemuganhu inobva pa `0` kusvika pa `10`.
Box rinotanga risina muganhu, zvireva kuti rinotanga riine `mg-sz-0`. 
Kana uchida kuchinja `color` yemuganhu, unoshandisa `mg-c-`, zvichireva kuti `muganhu color`

<DuoImage source1="/img/box13.jpg" source2="/img/box13.1.jpg"/>


:::info chii chikuitika apa
- Taumba ma `box` 3 ane zvakasiyana siyana
- Taisa miganhu pama `box` aya ine ma `size` akasiyana uye ma `color` akasiyana.
:::
