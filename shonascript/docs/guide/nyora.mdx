## Nyora Chii?
Nyora i `function` yaunotuma kuita `basa` rekunyora zvinhu mu`terminal`. Unozonzwisisa zvema `function` kana waverenga nezvawo kumberi, asi panguva ino chimbongoziva kuti ma `function` anoita `basa` raanenge atumwa.


## Mashandisirwo a `nyora()`


<Image source="/img/nyora1.jpeg"/>

Chii chikuitika apa?
Nyora anotumwa zvekunyora zvacho zviri muma brackets _()_.
Tichatarisa `line` rimwe ne rimwe tichiedza kunzwisisa kuti zvii zvikuitika. Ma `line` atiri kutaura ndiwo ari mu picture iri pamusoro apa

**Line 1**
```
nyora("Hesi")
```
Apa `nyora()` apihwa mashoko ekunyora, mashoko acho ndi "Hesi". "Hesi" apa anotariswa ne `computer` semashoko nekuti akaiswa mumaquotes _""_. Kana computer ichinge yatumwa kunyora mashoko, inongonyora mashoko sezvaari.  Saka Ukateedzera ma steps aya wega: 

:::info Masteps ekushandisa `nyora()`
1. mu file rakanzi `index.shona`, nyora kuti `nyora("Hesi")`
2. mu `terminal` nyora kuti `node shona.js`, wobaya `enter`
3. mu `terminal` zvekare, nyora kuti `node index.js`, wobaya `enter`
:::
Ukaita masteps aya zvakanaka, unofana kuona muterminal makanyorwa kuti `Hesi`. Makorokoto, wagona kushandisa `nyora()`

**Line 2**
```
nyora(2021)
```
Apa nyora apihwa `nhamba` yekunyora. `Nhamba` yacho ndi `2021`.

<Question text='Computer inozvinona sei kuti inhamba? Ko sei usina kuisa ma quotes kuti `nyora("2021")`?' />

`Computer` inozviona kuti i `nhamba` kana iri `nhamba` chaiyo isina kuiswa muma quotes. Uchazonzwisisa tsananguro yema _quotes_ ukaramba uchiverenga. Panguva ino dzokorora ma steps 3 ambotaurwa pamusoro apo, asi nyora kuti `nyora(2021)` panzvimbo ya `nyora("Hesi")`. 
Unofana kuona kuti 2021 mu `terminal`

**Line 4 na Line 5**
```
zitaRangu = "Panashe"
nyora(zitaRangu)
```

Apa tatanga nekugadzira `variable`. Kana usati waakunzwisisa kuti `variable` chii, unogona kuverenga nezvawo kumashure kune _topic_ yema `variable`. `Variable` yagadzirwa apa ndi `zitaRangu`, ichibva yanzi ndi `"Panashe"`. Unogona kubvisa pakanzi "Panashe", oisa zita rako riri muma _quotes_. Usasiye maquotes, nekuti hazvishande.

Izvi zvinoreva kuti tikazotuma `computer` kunotsvaga `variable` inonzi `zitaRangu`, inofana kudzoka na `"Panashe"`. Saka pa `line 5`, pakuudza computer kuti `nyora(zitaRangu)`, ichaona kuti `nyora()` uye apihwa zvekunyora, asi zvekunyora zvacho hazvisi kuma _quotes_, saka haasi mashoko. `Computer` ichatarisa kuti kana asiri mashoko, saka i `nhamba` here? Kwete. 

Saka zvaakureva kuti nyora apihwa `variable`.
`Variable` ichatsvagwa kumashure kuti wamboigadzira here. Saka apa, hongu, waigadzira `zitaRangu = "Panashe"` pa `line 4`. Ukaita ma steps 3 aye ekumashure uku, unofana kuona mu `terminal` makanzi `Panashe`.

**Line 7**

Sekutsanangurwa kwaitwa pana `line 4 na line 5`, apa `nyora()` apihwa zvekunyora, asi zvekunyora zvacho i `variable`. `Computer` ichatsvaga `variable` inonzi `zitaRake` kumusoro kwe code yako, yobva yaishaya. Saka ukada kudzokorora ma steps 3 aye akumashure uku, uchona kuti pauchanyora mu terminal kuti `node shona.js`, uchanzi:
:::danger Paita Problem
Paita problem, `zitaRake` haasi kuzivikanwa paline 7
:::

## Zvinobvumidzwa muna `nyora()`
Saka zvii zvinobvumidzwa muma _bracket_ a `nyora()`.

:::info Zvinobvumidzwa muma brackets 
1. **mashoko**
- zvinhu zvese zvaiswa muma quotes chero zvisingareve chinhu zvinotariswa ne computer se mashoko
2. **nhamba**
- nhamba inonyorwa iri _one_. Kuti `nyora(20 21)` hazviite nekuti 20 na 21 atove ma `nhamba` akasiyan ari 2. Uchazoona pazasi kuti kana uchida kunyora zvinhu zvakasiyana siyana muma brackets a `nyora()` unoita sei
3. **ma** `variables`
- kana usati waakunzwisisa kuti ma varibles chii, enda kumashure kune _topic_ yema `varables` kuti ugonzwisisa
:::

## Kupa `nyora()` zvakawanda

<Question text="Ko kana uchida kuti nyora() anyore zvakawanda, unoita sei?"/>

Kunyora zvakawanda kwatiri kutaura apa ndiko kutuma nyora kuti anyore zvakawana panguva imwe chete. 
Ngatimboti iwewe ukuda kuti `nyora()` anyore ma nhamba 2. 
Manhamba acho ndi 2021 na 2022, unonyora sei?

<Image source="/img/nyora3.jpeg"/>

Chii chikuitika apa?

Kana uchida kuti `nyora()` anyore zvakawanda, unoisa ma `comma,` pakati pezvinhu zvacho zvaukuda kuti anyore;

**Line 1**

```
nyora(2021,2022)
```
Apa, `nyora()` apihwa zvinhu 2; `nhamba` 2021 na `nhamba` 2022. Ma `nhamba` pakati pema `nhamba` aya pane ma comma, zvichireva kuti ukuudza `computer` kuti `2021` na `2022` ma `nhamba` akasiyana. 

Ukasaisa `comma`, Ukanyora kuti

:::danger Hazvibvumidzwe
```
nyora(2021 2022)
```
:::
`Computer` ichatadza kunzwisisa kuti `2021 2022` chii, nekuti haisi `nhamba`, `mashoko`, kana `variable`. Saka icahkuudza kuti `paita problem`

**Line 2 na Line 3**

Apa tatanga nekugadzira `variable` inonzi `zitaRangu`. Saka paline 5, ukupa `nyora()` zvinhu 2; mashoko ekuti `"Inini ndinonzi "`, ne `variable` inonzi `zitaRangu`. 
Ukaita ma steps 3 aye ekumashure emu `terminal`, unoona kuti `Inini ndinonzi Panashe` mu terminal.
