# Enzaniso

## Enzaniso chii?

:::caution Not yet Revised
:::


`Enzaniso` dzinoshandiswa kana uchida ku`enzanisa` ma `variables` 2. 
Ngatomboti une 2 ma`variables` awagadzira kare `variableYekutanga` na `variableYechipiri`.
Kana uchishandisa `enzaniso`, unenge uchitora `variable` yekutanga, oi`enzanisa` ne imwe `variable`. Pane `enzaniso` dzemhando 3.

Apa, unogona kutora `variableYekutanga` uchida kuona zvinhu 3 izvi:

:::info Mhando yeenzaniso
1. **Yakafanana na** `variableYechipiri` here?
2. **Idiki pane** `variableYechipiri` here?
3. **Ihombe kudarika/kupfuura** `variableYechipiri` here?
:::

## Mashandisirwo e `enzaniso`

Mashandisirwo chaiwo e `enzaniso` uchazomaoona kana waverenga _topic_ ye `Kana, Zvimwe`. Asi panguva ino unogona kumboona zvinoitika panenge pachishandiswa `enzaniso`.

:::info Tsananguro
**Line 1 na Line 2**
```
variableYekutanga = 5
variableYechipiri = 6
```
Tatanga nekugadzira ma `variable` 2:`variableYekutanga` na `variableYechipiri`. Ndiwo ma `variable` atiri kuda kuzo`enzanisa`

**Line 4 na Line 5**
```
zvakafananaHere = variableYekutanga yakafanana na variableYechipiri
nyora(zvakafananaHere)
```

Apa tiri kuda kuona kuti `variableYekutanga` yakafanana na `variableYechipiri` here, saka tashandisa `yakafanana na`. 
Ukaenzanisa ma `variable` aya, unofana kuona kuti akaenzana here kana kuti kwete. 

<Question text="ko nyora() arikutsvagei pa line 5"/>

pa `line 4` tanga tichienzanisa `variableYekutanga` na `variableYechipiri`, pakuenzanisa kwatiri kuita, tinogona kuona kuti `ichokwadi` kana kuti `kunyepa`.
Kuti iwewe ugone kuzoona kuti chii chaitiki pataenzanisa, uchashandisa `nyora()`.

Tagadzira `variable` inonzi `zvakafananaHere` tichibva taita kuti variable iyoyo iite zvinhu zvichabuda pakuenzanisa ma `variableYekutanga` na `variableYechipiri`. 
Tinoita izvi nekuti tikuda kuzonyora `variable` iyoyo tione kuti chii chaitika pataenzanisa.

:::

Chiita izvi wega unone zvinoitika

:::success Ita
1. mu file rakanzi `index.shona`
    nyora:
    ```
    variableYekutanga = 5
    variableYechipiri = 6
    zvakafananaHere = variableYekutanga akafanana na variableYechipiri
    nyora(zvakafananaHere)
    ```
2. mu `terminal` nyora kuti `node shona.js`, wobaya `enter`
3. mu `terminal` zvekare, nyora kuti `node index.js`, wobaya `enter`
:::


Ukaita masteps aya zvakanaka, unofana kuona mu terminal makanzi
```
false
```
Zvichireva kuti `kunyepa`. Ukatarisawo `variableYekutanga` ndi 5, na `variableYechipiri` ndi 6 havana kuenzana saka zvechokwadi kuti _5 akafanana na 6_ **kunyepa**.

:::info Tsananguro
**Line 7 na Line 8**

Matsanangurirwo aitwa pa **line 4 na line 5** akafanana ne matsanangurirwo epa **line 7 na line 8**. Asi changochinja ndechekuti tiri kuda kuona kuti `variableYekutanga` idiki here pana `variableYechipiri`. 
Ukadzokorora **masteps 3** arimu _box re green_ rakanzi **Ita**, unofana kuona mu `terminal` makanzi:
```
true
```
`true` zvireva kuti `ichokwadi`. Zvechokwadiwo `variableYekutanga`(5), idiki pane `variableYechipiri`(6)


**Line 10 na Line 11**

Matsanangurirwo aitwa pa **line 4 na line 5** akafanana ne matsanangurirwo epa **line 7 na line 8**. Asi changochinja ndechekuti tiri kuda kuona kuti `variableYekutanga` ihombe here pana `variableYechipiri`. 
Ukadzokorora **masteps 3** arimu _box re green_ rakanzi **Ita**, unofana kuona mu `terminal` makanazi:
```
false
```
`False` zvireva kuti kunyepa. Zvechokwadi `variableYekutanga`(5), haasi muhombe pane `variableYechipiri` (6)

:::
