# Ma Variables


## Variable Chii?
Kuti unzwisise kuti ma variables chii, ndichapa muenzaniso we <Highlight color="#008080">hembe yaunofarira</Highlight> . Ngatimboti iwewe une hembe yaunofarira, hembe iyoyo `ndeye red`. Iwewe ukuda kutuma mumwe munhu asingazive nezvehembe dzako kunotora hembe yaunofarira kwayakarongedzwa. Ukangoudza munhu uyu, kuti _â€œenda kunotora hembe yandofariraâ€_, munhu uyu achakubvunza kuti `hembe yaunofarira` yakaita sei, nekuti akaenda kunoitsvaga iye asingaizive haamboiwane.

Uchishandisa muenzaniso uyu, munhu wauri kutuma i `computer`.

### Ngatimbotarisa ku **left** kwe equal sign (=).

Tanyora ma words 2 epa  `hembe yandinofarira` akabatana kuita `hembeYandinofarira`. Manyorero aya anonzi `lower camel casing`. Unogona kuverenga nezvayo [pano.](https://www.computerhope.com/jargon/c/camelcase.htm)
Kana tichinyora ma variables, ku left kwemabrackets kunoiswa word one rakabatanidzwa. Izvi ndizvo zvinonzwisiswa ne computer. Unogona kubatanidza ma words aye kuita `hembe_yandinofarira` kana kuti `HembeYandinofarira` kana kuti `hembeyandinofarira`. 

Ukaisa ma words 2 kuti `hembe yandinofarira = "ndeye red"`, computer ichafunga kuti ukuedza kunyora two ma variables `hembe` na `yandinofarira` panguva imwe chete, saka inokuudza kuti manyorero awaita `hembe yandinofarira haasiriwo`. Tarisa mamwe manyorero asinganzwisiswe ne computer mu box re red pazasi

:::danger Manyorero ekuleft side asingaite
`hembe yandinofarira = "ndeye red"` -> ku left kunofana kuenda one word

`1hembeYandinofarira = "ndeye red"` -> usatange zita revariable ne number kana !@#$%^&*

`hembe-yandinofarira = "ndeye red"` -> usaise hyphen(-) muzita re variable, asi unogona kushandisa underscore (_) kubatanidza ma words kuita one sezvambotaurwa
:::


:::success Manyorero ekuleft side anoita
`hembeYandinofarira = "ndeye red"` 

`HembeYandinofarira = "ndeye red"`

`hembe_yandinofarira = "ndeye red"`
:::

### Ngatichitarisa ku **right** kwe equal sign (=)

Chekutanga, unoona kuti pane ma _quotes_ (""). Zvinhu zvese zviri mu ma quotes zvinoverengwa ne computer se chinhu one, sema words, uye zvinoverengwa **sezvazviri**. Ukasaisa ma quotes ukati `hembeYandinofarira = ndeye red`, computer ichatarisa kuti chinhu chiri kuright kwe equal sign (=) ichiedza kuda kuona kuti:
:::caution Chii chiri ku right?
- i `nhamba` here?  - **haisi**
- imwe `code` ine zvainoreva zvinozivikanwa ne Shonascript here? - **haisi**
> code ine zvainoreva iri kutaurwa apa uchazoiziva kumberi ukaramba uchiverenga
:::
Saka kana computer yaona kuti ku right hakuna zvinhu izvi, ichaona sokuti zvese zviri kuright i **variable**. Asi ku right kune 2 ma words, saka ichaona kunge urikuda kunyora 2 ma variables panguva imwe chete yokuudza kuti `manyorero awaita haasiriwo`. Asi kana kuright kwacho kuine word one, sokuti `hembeYandinofarira = red`, computer ichaona kuti waisa variable ku right, yoedza kutsvaga kuti variable inonzi `red` yambogadzirwa here mu code yako. 
:::note Kugadzira variable kuita sei?
 Kugadzira variable ndiko kuti `zitaReVariable = "zvimwe zvinhu"` zvakafanana nekuti `hambeYandinofarira = "ndeye red"`. Apa panenge pachigadzirwa variable inonzi `hembeYandinofarira`
:::

Kana computer ikashaya variable inonzi red yairi kutsvaga, inonyora kuti `variable inonzi red haisi kuzivikanwa`.
