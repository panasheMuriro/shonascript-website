## Variable Chii?
Kuti unzwisise kuti ma variables chii, ndichapa muenzaniso we <Highlight color="#008080">hembe yaunofarira</Highlight> . Ngatimboti iwewe une hembe yaunofarira, hembe iyoyo `ndeye red`. Iwewe ukuda kutuma mumwe munhu asingazive nezvehembe dzako kunotora hembe yaunofarira kwayakarongedzwa. Ukangoudza munhu uyu, kuti _‚Äúenda kunotora hembe yandofarira‚Äù_, munhu uyu achakubvunza kuti `hembe yaunofarira` yakaita sei, nekuti akaenda kunoitsvaga iye asingaizive haamboiwane. 

:::info Tsananguro

- Uchishandisa muenzaniso uyu, munhu wauri kutuma i `computer`.

- Iwewe ndiwe munhu ari kuda kunyora code ne **Shonascript** kunotsvaga `hembe yaunofarira`.
- **Variable** apa i `hembe yaunofarira`, `hembe yaunofarira` yacho `‚Äúndeye red‚Äù`
:::

## Manyorerwo ema variables

Saka unotaura sei ne`computer` nezve `hembe yaunofarira`? Unofana kunyora **code**  inotaura ne `computer` seizvi:

:::info Ngatimbotarisa ku **left** kwe equal sign (=).

Tanyora ma _words_ 2 epa  `hembe yandinofarira` akabatana kuita `hembeYandinofarira`. Manyorero aya anonzi `lower camel casing`. Unogona kuverenga nezvayo [pano.](https://www.computerhope.com/jargon/c/camelcase.htm)
Kana tichinyora ma variables, ku _left_ kwema_brackets_ kunoiswa _word one_ rakabatanidzwa. Izvi ndizvo zvinonzwisiswa ne `computer`. Unogona kubatanidza ma words aye kuita `hembe_yandinofarira` kana kuti `HembeYandinofarira` kana kuti `hembeyandinofarira`. 

Ukaisa ma _words_ 2 kuti `hembe yandinofarira = "ndeye red"`, computer ichafunga kuti ukuedza kunyora two ma `variables` `hembe` na `yandinofarira` panguva imwe chete, saka inokuudza kuti manyorero awaita `hembe yandinofarira haasiriwo`. Tarisa mamwe manyorero asinganzwisiswe ne `computer` mu box re `red` pazasi

:::

:::danger Manyorero ekuleft side asingaite
`hembe yandinofarira = "ndeye red"` -> ku left kunofana kuenda _one word_

`1hembeYandinofarira = "ndeye red"` -> usatange zita re`variable` ne _number_ kana !@#$%^&*

`hembe-yandinofarira = "ndeye red"` -> usaise _hyphen(-)_ muzita re `variable`, asi unogona kushandisa _underscore (_)_ kubatanidza ma _words_ kuita one sezvambotaurwa
:::


:::success Manyorero ekuleft side anoita
`hembeYandinofarira = "ndeye red"` 

`HembeYandinofarira = "ndeye red"`

`hembe_yandinofarira = "ndeye red"`
:::

:::info Ngatichitarisa ku **right** kwe equal sign (=)

Chekutanga, unoona kuti pane ma _quotes_ (""). Zvinhu zvese zviri mu ma _quotes_ zvinoverengwa ne `computer` se chinhu chimwe chete, semashoko, uye zvinoverengwa **sezvazviri**. Ukasaisa ma _quotes_ ukati `hembeYandinofarira = ndeye red`, `computer` ichatarisa kuti chinhu chiri _kuright_ kwe _equal sign (=)_ ichiedza kuda kuona kuti:

1. i `nhamba` here?  - **haisi**
2. imwe `code` ine zvainoreva zvinozivikanwa ne `Shonascript` here? - **haisi**
> `code` ine zvainoreva iri kutaurwa apa uchazoiziva kumberi ukaramba uchiverenga

Saka kana `computer` yaona kuti ku _right_ hakuna zvinhu izvi, ichaona sokuti zvese zviri _kuright_ i `variable`. Asi ku _right_ kune 2 mashoko aisira muma _quotes_, saka ichaona kunge urikuda kunyora 2 ma `variables` panguva imwe chete yokuudza kuti `manyorero awaita haasiriwo`. Asi kana _kuright_ kwacho kuine _word_ rimwe chete, sokuti `hembeYandinofarira = red`, computer ichaona kuti waisa `variable` ku `right`, yoedza kutsvaga kuti `variable` inonzi `red` yambogadzirwa here mu `code` yako. 
<Question text="Kugadzira variable kuita sei?"/>

Kugadzira variable ndiko kuti `zitaReVariable = "zvimwe zvinhu"` zvakafanana nekuti `hambeYandinofarira = "ndeye red"`. Apa panenge pachigadzirwa `variable` inonzi `hembeYandinofarira`

Kana `computer` ikashaya `variable` inonzi `red` yairi kutsvaga, inonyora kuti `variable inonzi red haisi kuzivikanwa`.
:::
## Unoona sei kuti variable yako yadzirwa?

:::info kunyora uchishandisa `nyora()`
Pane anonzi ma _functions_, auchazoziva pauchaverenga nezve _topic yema functions_. Munhu anoshandisa ma _functions_ kana achida kutuma `computer` kuti iite `basa`. `Shonascript` ine _function_ inoita `basa` rekunyora zvaukuda kuona inonzi `nyora()`. 
Enda kumberi kana uchida kuona kuti `nyora()` anoshanda sei. Izvi zvichakubatsira kunzwisisa mashandiro ema `variables`. 
:::
